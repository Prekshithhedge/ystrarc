About Us

Welcome to YSTR

Where we empower structural solutions with expertise, innovation, and reliability. As a leading provider of structural engineering services, we are committed to delivering exceptional results that enhance safety, efficiency, and sustainability across diverse projects and industries.

Empowering Through Education

In addition to our services, YSTR is committed to empowering the next generation of structural engineers through comprehensive training programs. Through hands-on workshops, seminars, and online courses, we provide aspiring engineers with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in the field, fostering innovation and excellence in structural engineering practices.

Building the Future, Together

Join us on a journey of innovation and collaboration as we embark on building the future, together. At YSTR, we believe in the power of partnership, working hand in hand with our clients to create sustainable, resilient structures that leave a lasting impact on communities and the environment.

Bio

Ramprakash

Dr. N Ramprakash, a luminary in civil and structural engineering, holds a Bachelor of Engineering from Bangalore University and a Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science. His prolific publications and active participation in international conferences underscore his scholarly eminence and global influence.

In the professional sphere, Dr. Ramprakash's leadership is evident through roles such as past president of the Association of Consulting Civil Engineers (INDIA) and Vice President of the Indian Concrete Institute. His founding of INSTRUCT and governance in SEWC(India) highlight his visionary contributions to the field.

Dr. Ramprakash's portfolio showcases groundbreaking projects like the RAVIKIRAN APARTMENTS and innovative designs such as the 88-meter Tubular Double Layer Grid Lamella Geodesic Dome. His expertise spans industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects, reflecting a rare fusion of creativity and technical prowess.
