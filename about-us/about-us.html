About the Founder

Bio

Emily Johnson

Emily Johnson is a visionary interior designer known for her ability to transform spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments. With a background in architecture and a passion for design, Emily brings a unique perspective to every project she undertakes.

Before establishing herself as a leading figure in the interior design industry, Emily pursued her education at prestigious institutions such as Parsons School of Design. Inspired by her travels and experiences, she blends different styles and influences to create spaces that are both timeless and contemporary.

Emily's journey in interior design began when she recognized the power of spaces to influence emotions and behaviors. She believes that every space has the potential to evoke feelings of comfort, inspiration, and joy. Her dedication to understanding her clients' needs and aspirations ensures that each project reflects their personality and lifestyle.
